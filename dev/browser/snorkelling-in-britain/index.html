Awesome weekend spent on a @cornwallwildlifetrust Seasearch course run by the inspiring and informative @sea_loop and @mary.ledlie. Thoroughly recommend this training for anyone who snorkels/freedives/dives - not only will it make your time in the water more rewarding in terms of identifying what you spot but it the data gathered is so important in informing our understanding of the marine environment around our coasts and how it is changing. After a day of theory on the Saturday we hit the water on Sunday which was warming up nicely and we were treated to pretty good visibility. Mary patiently helped me to identify some seaweeds (which I have always found tricky) and did a great job of rescuing some of the orange-clubbed sea slugs we found floating around (perhaps having fallen off their homes during the stormy weather) :-( The weekend was topped off by the treat of getting to sit around in the sunshine chatting to the super inspiring people with us on the course like @blue_embrace_freediving and @nara_do_mar and @keithyraven Thanks so much all and hope to see you in the water again soon!
