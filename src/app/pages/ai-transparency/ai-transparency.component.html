AI Transparency

At Snorkelology, we take transparency seriously. Some of the descriptions for snorkelling organisations on our map have been produced with the assistance of large language models (LLMs), reviewed and supervised by our team. We do this to bring useful information to the map more quickly, but a human always has the final say before anything is published.

AI-generated content is clearly signposted on the map with an "About this content" link. Where a description has been written or reviewed by our team directly, no such label is shown.

If you spot a mistake, or if you're an organisation that would prefer not to be included, please get in touch. We'll put it right promptly.
