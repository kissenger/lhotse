What is Snorkelology?

Hello and welcome to Snorkelology! We are Emma and Gordon Taylor, snorkelling and SCUBA instructors with more than 20 years experience exploring the British coastline.

We are authors of Snorkelling Britain, a new guide to the best snorkel sites around the country.

Explore our website for British snorkelling sites, snorkelling gear tips and recommendations, inspiring underwater photography, and many more snorkelling resources...
