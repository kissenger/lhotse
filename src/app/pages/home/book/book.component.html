Your essential guide to snorkelling in Britain! Buying from snorkelology means buying direct from the authors and supporting the three years of painstaking research that went into its creation.

Snorkelling Britain: 100 Marine Adventures by Emma and Gordon Taylor — Your essential guide to British snorkelling.

Packed with detailed route-finding advice, inspirational photography and a comprehensive introduction covering snorkelling safety, sustainable snorkelling, getting started and essential gear, Snorkelling Britain is the definitive guide to where to snorkel around the UK coastline. A perfect gift for any snorkeller or open water swimmer who wants to take the next step on their marine adventures.

100 British Snorkelling Sites

  • Sites for all abilities — from family-friendly rock pools to advanced open-water snorkels
  • Rocky reefs, shipwrecks, kelp forests, tidal pools and sandy beaches
  • Detailed directions, parking and access information for every site
  • Tailored safety advice including tides, currents and hazards
  • Covering the best snorkelling locations in England, Scotland and Wales

Packed with Supporting Info

  • Comprehensive introduction covering planning, equipment, safety and underwater photography
  • Foreword by Sophie Rennie, BSAC's National Diving Officer and Head of Snorkelling
  • Illustrated guide to British marine life and species identification
  • Ten curated 'Best For' lists including Urban Access, Tidal Pools, Families and Beginners
